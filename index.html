Frequently Asked

Common Questions, Answered

Is dental implant treatment painful?

No. Implant placement is done under local anaesthesia, so the procedure itself is painless. Mild soreness for a day or two afterward is normal and managed easily with medication.

How long does an implant last?

With proper oral hygiene and regular check-ups, a dental implant can last 15–20 years, and often a lifetime.

How many sittings are needed for RCT?

Most root canal treatments are completed in 1–2 sittings, depending on the condition and infection level of the tooth.

What is the cost of dental implants?

Cost depends on the implant brand, number of implants, and bone condition. We provide a transparent, written quotation after consultation — no hidden charges.

Do you provide cosmetic dentistry?

Yes — we offer smile designing, teeth whitening, veneers, and complete cosmetic dental makeovers tailored to your face and goals.

How often should I visit a dentist?

We recommend a check-up every 6 months for preventive care and early detection of any issues.
